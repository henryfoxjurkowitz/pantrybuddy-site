The perfect app for any cook, at any level, all the time.

I designed this app with the original purpose of helping the Community Cooks program in my town find new meals to make. But I hope anyone that comes across it finds it helpful!

Pricing

It's totally free! I'm only hoping to help people find recipes (a problem I'm very familiar with). So download it now - what are you waiting for!?

Use

First, in the "Pantry" tab add the foods that you have in stock to your virtual pantry.

If there are any foods you want to add that aren't in the search options, it's easy to add them yourself!

Then, go to the "Search" tab. Add any keywords to narrow your search, and press "Get My Recipes!" It's as easy as that.

Look through the recipes for something that catches your eye. Then, you can tap it to find out it's specific ingredients. If it sounds good, click the link and start cooking!

Feel free to use the bookmark button to save recipes. They will be saved under the "Saved" tab so that you can come back to them later.

Also use the "Saved" tab to view or remove any custom foods that you have added to your possible search results.
